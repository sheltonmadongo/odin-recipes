Easy Creamy Mushroom Stew

Delicious mushroom side dish

This creamy mushroom stew is delicius and rich in flavour. It is super easy and quick to cook. It is a great side dish that goes with pretty much any meal or can be enjoyed on it's own.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons chicken broth
  • 1 (8 ounce) can canned mushrooms
  • ½ cup heavy whipping cream
  • ½ teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon tumeric
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add mushrooms and saute until tender, 4 to 5 minutes. Add garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 more minute.
  2. Add chicken broth and simmer for 3 to 4 minutes.
  3. Pour in heavy whipping cream and season with paprika, tumeric, salt, and pepper. Simmer until stew starts to thicken, 4 to 5 minutes.
